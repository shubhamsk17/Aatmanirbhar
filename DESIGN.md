## âœ¨ Interactions

### Hover Effects
- Cards: Lift up slightly + shadow increases
- Buttons: Scale up (105%), background darkens
- Links: Color change + underline animation
- Navigation: Underline slide effect

### Transitions
- All: 300ms ease
- Smooth, professional feel
- No jarring animations

### Mobile Menu
- Slides in from top
- Fade animation
- Click-outside to close
- Links auto-close menu

## ğŸ¯ Design Principles

1. **Clean & Modern**: Minimal clutter, ample whitespace
2. **Professional**: Business-appropriate design
3. **Indian Context**: Orange/saffron and green colors (flag colors)
4. **Accessible**: High contrast, readable fonts
5. **Engaging**: Subtle animations, interactive elements
